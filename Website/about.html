About Us

Built by Players, For Champions

Our Story

Tufani Tournament was born in 2023 from a simple observation: Indian Free Fire players deserved a real tournament platform. Existing platforms were slow to pay winners, used outdated anti-cheat systems, and treated players like numbers rather than competitors.

Founded by a team of esports enthusiasts and software engineers from Bengaluru, Tufani set out to build the tournament platform we wished existed — one with instant prize withdrawals, genuine anti-cheat, and real community engagement. Within six months of launch, we crossed 1 lakh registered players. Today, we serve over 5 lakh tournament players across India.

Our Mission

To make Free Fire tournaments accessible, fair, and rewarding for every Indian player — whether you're a rising competitor in a Tier-3 city or a ranked pro from a major metro.

Our Values

Fair Play First

Zero tolerance for cheaters. Our AI anti-cheat system ensures every tournament match is decided by pure skill.

Radical Transparency

Results, payouts, and match data are all verifiable. What you see is what you get — no hidden deductions.

Community Driven

Every tournament feature we build is shaped by player feedback. Our Discord runs on suggestions, not commands.

Player Prosperity

We succeed only when you do. Same-day withdrawals, low entry fees, and the highest prize pools in Indian mobile esports.
