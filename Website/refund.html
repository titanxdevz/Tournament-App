Refunds

Refund Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

1. Tournament Entry Fees

Entry fees for tournaments are non-refundable once a tournament match has started. If you cancel your participation before the tournament begins, the entry fee will be credited back to your Tufani tournament wallet as bonus cash (not withdrawable).

2. Tournament Cancellation by Tufani

If a tournament is cancelled by Tufani due to insufficient participation or technical issues, all entry fees are fully refunded to your withdrawable wallet balance within 24 hours.

3. Deposit Refunds

Wallet deposits are generally non-refundable since they represent stored value for gameplay. However, if a deposit was made due to a technical error or unauthorized transaction, contact support within 48 hours for review.

4. Disputed Match Results

If you believe a tournament match result was incorrectly recorded, submit a dispute through the app within 6 hours of match completion. Our review team will investigate and, if the result is found to be erroneous, we will either reissue the match or refund the entry fee.

5. Withdrawal Reversals

Once a withdrawal request has been submitted and processed, it cannot be reversed. Ensure your UPI ID or bank details are correct before requesting a withdrawal.

6. Account Closure

When you close your account, any remaining withdrawable balance will be transferred to your registered bank account after verification (processing time: 5-7 business days). Bonus cash and promotional balances are forfeited upon account closure.

7. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider will result in immediate account suspension. If you have a legitimate concern, please contact our support team first — we resolve 95% of disputes within 6 hours.

8. Contact for Refunds

For any refund-related queries, email support@tufanitournament.gg with your registered mobile number and transaction details. We aim to respond within 4 hours.
