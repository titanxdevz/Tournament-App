Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, improve site performance, and deliver relevant content.

2. How We Use Cookies

Tufani Tournament's tournament platform uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential: Required for the platform to function (login sessions, security)
  • Analytics: Help us understand how users interact with the platform
  • Preference: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing: Deliver relevant promotions (with your consent)

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Category Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain your login state during a browsing session Session
Persistent Cookies Remember your preferences across visits 30 days - 1 year
Analytics Cookies Track page views, feature usage, and performance 1-2 years
Third-Party Cookies Payment gateways and embedded content Varies

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services such as Razorpay (payments), Google Analytics (analytics), and Cloudflare (CDN) that may set their own cookies. These are governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes take effect immediately upon posting. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

7. Contact

For questions about cookies, contact us at support@tufanitournament.gg.
