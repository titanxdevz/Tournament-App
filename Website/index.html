Got Questions

We've Got Answers

If something's still unclear after reading, hit us on Discord — we reply fast.

Ask on Discord
Is Tufani Tournament legal in India?
Yes. Tufani operates as a skill-based gaming platform, which is legally recognized in most Indian states. We are registered with the Fantasy Sports Federation of India and comply with all applicable laws. We do not operate in Assam, Odisha, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Nagaland, Sikkim, and Tamil Nadu where such platforms are restricted.
How do I verify my results were counted correctly?
All match results are pulled directly from Garena's official Free Fire game server API. Your in-game UID is linked to your Tufani account, so every kill, placement, and stat is verified at source. You can view your full match breakdown in the app within 90 seconds of a match ending.
How quickly can I withdraw my winnings?
Withdrawals requested before 6 PM are processed the same day, typically within 2–4 hours. Weekend withdrawals are processed first thing Monday. Minimum withdrawal is ₹100, and there are no fees for withdrawals above ₹500.
What happens if someone cheats in my match?
Our AI flags suspicious patterns automatically. If a cheat is confirmed within 24 hours of a match, the prize pool is redistributed to legitimate participants. You can also report players directly in the app, and human reviewers act on credible reports within 6 hours.
Is there a minimum age requirement?
You must be 18 years or older to participate in cash tournaments and make deposits or withdrawals. Age verification is performed during KYC. Players under 18 can browse and play practice matches, but cannot enter cash contests.
How do I join a tournament?
Go to the Tournaments section, browse active tournaments, and tap "Join Tournament" on any open event. Ensure your wallet has enough balance for the entry fee. You'll receive lobby details and match instructions immediately after joining.
What happens if I disconnect mid-match?
If you disconnect during a tournament match, your stats are still recorded based on your in-game performance up to the point of disconnection. We recommend a stable internet connection. Frequent disconnections may result in a temporary cooldown to protect fair play.
How is my skill rating calculated?
Your skill rating uses a modified ELO system that factors in kills, placement, damage dealt, and survival time across your last 50 matches. New players start at 1200 rating. Win rates above 60% will see faster rating growth.
Can I play from restricted states?
Tufani Tournament does not operate in Assam, Odisha, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Nagaland, Sikkim, and Tamil Nadu due to local regulations. If you attempt to register from these states, the system will block participation in cash tournaments. Practice matches remain accessible.
