Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Tufani Tournament Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our tournament platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and username
  • Identity Verification: PAN card, Aadhaar (for KYC), and selfie for age/identity verification
  • Tournament Data: Free Fire UID, tournament match history, kills, wins, placement history, and performance statistics
  • Financial Data: UPI ID, bank account details (for withdrawals), and transaction history
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, device model, OS version, app usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process tournament entries and distribute prizes
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Improve our platform and matchmaking systems
  • Communicate important updates and promotional offers (with consent)
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay, Paytm, etc.) for transaction processing
  • Game API providers (Garena) for match result verification
  • Government authorities as required by law
  • Service providers who help operate our platform (under strict NDA)

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account closure, data is retained for 3 years to comply with legal obligations, after which it is permanently deleted.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal holds)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Download a copy of your data

7. Security

We implement 256-bit SSL encryption, regular security audits, and strict access controls. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

8. Grievance Officer

For privacy-related complaints, contact our Grievance Officer at grievance@tufanitournament.gg. We resolve complaints within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Email: support@tufanitournament.gg
