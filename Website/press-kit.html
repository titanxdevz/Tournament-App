About Tufani Tournament

Tufani Tournament is India's fastest-growing Free Fire esports tournament platform. Founded in 2023, we've served over 5 lakh players, hosted 12,000+ tournaments, and distributed over 2 crore rupees in prize money. Our platform combines real-time anti-cheat, instant withdrawals, and skill-based matchmaking to create the fairest competitive environment in Indian mobile esports.

Brand Guidelines

Our brand colors are FF7A18 (blaze orange), FFD54F (gold), and 0B0F19 (dark void). Use the Tufani flame icon consistently across all materials. Do not stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo. Maintain clear space equal to the icon's width on all sides. When using the wordmark, keep it on dark backgrounds for maximum contrast.
