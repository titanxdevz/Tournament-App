Responsible Gaming

Play Smart, Play Safe

At Tufani Tournament, we believe competitive tournament play should be fun, rewarding, and always under your control. We're committed to providing a safe and responsible environment for every tournament player.

Know Your Limits

Skill-based gaming is entertainment, not a source of income. Never play with money you cannot afford to lose. If you or someone you know needs help, reach out to professional support organizations.

Our Responsible Gaming Tools

Self-Exclusion

Temporarily or permanently block yourself from participating in cash tournaments. Choose from 7-day, 30-day, or permanent exclusion periods.

Deposit Limits

Set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits. Once set, limits cannot be increased for 7 days. You can decrease them at any time.

Session Timers

Get notified after 1 hour, 2 hours, and 3 hours of continuous gameplay. Helps you stay aware of time spent on the platform.

Activity Dashboard

View your complete play history, deposits, withdrawals, and net spend. Full transparency to help you make informed decisions.

Signs of Problematic Play

If you experience any of the following, consider using our self-exclusion tools or seeking professional help:

  • Spending more time or money than you intended
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when unable to play
  • Chasing losses by entering more tournaments
  • Borrowing money or using funds meant for essential expenses
  • Lying to family or friends about your play activity
  • Neglecting work, studies, or relationships due to gaming

Age Restrictions

You must be at least 18 years old to participate in cash tournaments. Age verification is performed during KYC. Players under 18 can browse and play practice matches but cannot enter cash contests or make deposits.

Getting Help

If you or someone you know needs support, these organizations can help:

  • National Helpline (India): 1800-123-4455
  • Responsible Gaming Foundation: rgfindia.org
  • Tufani Support: support@tufanitournament.gg

Commitment

We regularly audit our tournament platform for compliance with responsible gaming standards. Our algorithms detect patterns that may indicate problematic play and automatically trigger safety measures. We train our support team to identify and assist players who may need help.

Gaming should lift you up, not weigh you down. If it stops being fun, step away. We'll be here when you're ready.
